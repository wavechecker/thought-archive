## Intro
Long COVID refers to persistent symptoms lasting weeks or months after infection with COVID-19.

It highlights a broader issue in medicine:
👉 Recovery does not always follow infection.

## Key Points
- Symptoms can last months after initial infection  
- Common symptoms: fatigue, brain fog, shortness of breath  
- Mechanism is not fully understood  
- Overlaps with other post-viral syndromes  

## Symptoms
- Fatigue  
- Cognitive dysfunction  
- Breathlessness  
- Chest pain  
- Sleep disturbance  

## Possible Causes
- Immune system dysregulation  
- Persistent inflammation  
- Autonomic dysfunction  
- Microvascular changes  

## Management
- Symptom-based treatment  
- Gradual return to activity  
- Multidisciplinary care  

## Prognosis
- Many improve over time  
- Some have prolonged symptoms  

## FAQ
**Q: Is long COVID permanent?**  
A: Most people improve, but recovery time varies.
