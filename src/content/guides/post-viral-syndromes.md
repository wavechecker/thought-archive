## Intro
Post-viral syndromes occur when symptoms persist after an infection has cleared.

They are increasingly recognised across multiple conditions, including:
- Lyme disease  
- COVID-19  
- Epstein-Barr virus  

## Key Points
- Symptoms persist after infection resolves  
- Not due to active infection in most cases  
- Mechanism is complex and not fully understood  
- Overlaps with chronic fatigue syndromes  

## Symptoms
- Fatigue  
- Brain fog  
- Pain  
- Sleep disturbance  

## Possible Mechanisms
- Immune dysregulation  
- Nervous system changes  
- Persistent inflammation  

## Management
- Symptom-based care  
- Gradual activity pacing  
- Supportive treatment  

## Prognosis
- Variable recovery  
- Some improve slowly over time  

## FAQ
**Q: Are post-viral syndromes psychological?**  
A: No — symptoms are real and have biological mechanisms, though not fully understood.
