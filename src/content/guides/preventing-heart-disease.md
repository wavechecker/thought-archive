Heart disease is the leading cause of death worldwide — but much of it is preventable.

**Lifestyle Steps:**
- Stop smoking
- Eat a balanced, heart-healthy diet
- Exercise regularly
- Maintain a healthy weight

**Medical Checks:**
- Blood pressure
- Cholesterol levels
- Blood glucose for diabetes
