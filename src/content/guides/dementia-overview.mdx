## Intro

Dementia is a progressive decline in memory and cognitive function.

It is not a normal part of aging.

---

## Key Points

- Alzheimer’s disease is the most common type  
- Vascular health plays a major role  
- Early symptoms are often subtle  
- Risk can be reduced  

---

## 🚨 Breakout

<div class="bg-yellow-50 border border-yellow-200 rounded-2xl p-4 my-6">
Memory loss is not the only sign.

Changes in behaviour, judgment, and personality can appear early.
</div>

---

## Prevention

- Control blood pressure  
- Manage diabetes  
- Stay physically and socially active
