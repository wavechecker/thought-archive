## Work With PatientGuide

PatientGuide is an evidence-based health information platform focused on delivering clear, trustworthy, and clinically responsible content for patients and readers.

We partner with a limited number of organisations for high-quality sponsored content and collaborations that align with our editorial standards.

---

## Our Approach

We are not a content marketplace.

Every collaboration is:

- Selective  
- Editorially reviewed  
- Aligned with our medical and public health standards  

Our priority is maintaining reader trust and long-term content quality.

---

## What We Offer

### Sponsored Articles

- Professionally reviewed and edited content  
- Integrated within our existing content ecosystem  
- Published for a fixed term (typically 6–12 months)  
- Clear disclosure and compliant link handling  

---

## What We Look For

We are open to collaborations involving:

- Health education and patient information  
- Medical technology and diagnostics  
- Digital health platforms  
- Preventative health and screening  
- Evidence-based wellness  

All content must provide real value to readers and meet our editorial standards.

---

## What We Do Not Accept

We do not work with content related to:

- Gambling or casino  
- CBD or unregulated substances  
- Adult content  
- Cryptocurrency promotions or financial schemes  
- Misleading or unverified medical claims  

---

## Editorial Standards

All sponsored content:

- Is subject to full editorial review  
- May be edited for clarity, accuracy, and tone  
- Must meet evidence-based standards  
- Can be rejected or removed at our discretion  

---

## Pricing

Pricing varies depending on scope, placement, and editorial involvement.

As a general guide:

- Sponsored article placements are typically offered on a 6-month basis  
- Editorial review and integration are included  

Final pricing is confirmed after reviewing the proposed content.
