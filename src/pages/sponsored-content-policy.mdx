## Sponsored Content Policy

PatientGuide accepts a limited number of sponsored articles and commercial collaborations.  
All sponsored content is subject to strict editorial, ethical, and quality standards to protect the integrity of our platform and our readers.

---

## Our Editorial Standard

PatientGuide is an evidence-based health information platform.

All content — including sponsored content — must:

- Be medically responsible and factually accurate  
- Avoid exaggerated, misleading, or unsupported claims  
- Align with public health principles and best available evidence  
- Provide genuine informational value to readers  

We do **not** publish content that is purely promotional or written solely for search engine manipulation.

---

## What We Accept

We may consider sponsored content related to:

- Health education and patient information  
- Medical technology and diagnostics  
- Digital health tools and platforms  
- Preventative health and screening  
- Evidence-based wellness topics  

All topics must be relevant to our audience and consistent with our existing content.

---

## What We Do NOT Accept

We do not accept sponsored content in the following categories:

- Gambling, casino, or betting-related content  
- CBD, unregulated substances, or grey-area health products  
- Adult content or explicit material  
- Cryptocurrency promotions or financial schemes  
- Content making unverified medical claims  
- Any topic that may harm patient safety or public trust  

Submissions that fall into these categories will be declined without review.

---

## Editorial Control

PatientGuide retains full editorial control over all sponsored content.

This includes the right to:

- Edit content for clarity, accuracy, tone, and structure  
- Request revisions prior to publication  
- Reject submissions that do not meet our standards  
- Remove published content at any time if it no longer meets our policies  

We prioritise reader trust over commercial considerations.

---

## Link Policy

All sponsored content links are handled in accordance with search engine guidelines.

- Paid links are marked with appropriate attributes (e.g. rel="sponsored" or nofollow)  
- We do not guarantee dofollow links  
- Excessive or irrelevant linking will be removed  

We do not participate in link schemes or bulk link placements.

---

## Disclosure

All sponsored content is clearly disclosed.

This may include a statement such as:

> This article is a sponsored collaboration and has been reviewed to meet PatientGuide’s editorial standards.

Transparency with our readers is essential.

---

## Content Duration

Sponsored content is published for a fixed term, typically:

- 6 months  
- or 12 months (by agreement)

At the end of the term, content may be:

- Removed  
- Archived  
- Renewed (subject to review and additional fee)

We do not guarantee permanent placement.

---

## Submission Process

To be considered, please provide:

- Proposed topic or article outline  
- Target links (if applicable)  
- Sample content or previous work  

All submissions are reviewed before acceptance.

---

## Payments

We currently accept payment in:

- USDC (ERC-20 / Ethereum network)

Details will be provided upon approval of content.

---

## Final Note

PatientGuide is not a content marketplace.

We are a curated health platform built on trust, accuracy, and long-term credibility.  
We reserve the right to decline any collaboration that does not align with these principles.
